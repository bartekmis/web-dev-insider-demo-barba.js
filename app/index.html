What is it?

It is a demo of a traditional, standard website with nice transitions when you navigate from one page to another. Content does not matter. Just click through the links to sub pages on this website. You will notice that pages are being loaded dynamically (sub pages), with transitions.
Like a real Maybach.

I guarantee that you can add the same effect under 30 min on your own website! Without any Angular, React or Vue.js!

You should compare this website with the static one here. Can you feel the difference?
